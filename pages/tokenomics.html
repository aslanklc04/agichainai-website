Utility

What AGIC is used for

Payments

AI inference, agent execution, API calls and marketplace purchases are all priced and settled in AGIC.

Staking

Validators and compute nodes lock AGIC as collateral. Honest work earns rewards; fraud is slashed.

Governance

One token, one vote in the AGIChainAI DAO — protocol upgrades, parameters and treasury spending.

Creator Rewards

Model creators and dataset publishers earn AGIC automatically each time their work is used.

Node Incentives

Operators providing GPU/CPU capacity receive AGIC for verified, correctly executed inference tasks.

Agent Economy

Autonomous agents hold and spend AGIC to pay for their own compute — enabling machine-to-machine commerce.

Governance

Community in control

AGIC holders govern the protocol through the AGIChainAI DAO. Voting power scales with staked tokens, and all proposals, discussions and results are recorded on-chain. Governance launches in phases — foundation-led, council, then full DAO — as described in the whitepaper.

Treasury

Transparent by default

The treasury address is public from day one. Funds move only through executed governance proposals, and quarterly transparency reports summarize inflows, outflows and runway for the entire community.
