Roadmap

Building the AGI Era,
step by step

Our development plan, quarter by quarter. Milestones may be refined as research and community feedback evolve — every change is announced publicly.

  1. Q3 2026 — Foundation

    Laying the groundwork

    • Official website launch at agichainai.com
    • Brand identity: logo, design system, messaging
    • Public GitHub organization and open repositories
    • Community channels: Telegram, Discord, X
  2. Q4 2026 — Whitepaper & Community

    Sharing the vision

    • Whitepaper v1.0 publication
    • Technical documentation portal (first version)
    • Ambassador & early-contributor program
    • First community AMA sessions
    • Developer community building: hackathon announcements
  3. Q1 2027 — Testnet, Token & Wallet

    First working network

    • Public testnet launch with Proof-of-Inference prototype
    • Smart contract audits by independent firms
    • AGIC token generation event (TGE)
    • Non-custodial wallet — beta release
    • Incentivized testnet campaign for node operators
  4. Q2 2027 — Marketplace

    Opening the AI economy

    • AI model & agent marketplace launch
    • Creator onboarding and automatic revenue sharing
    • Developer SDK v1.0 with public API
    • AGI Chat — public beta on distributed compute
  5. Q3 2027 — Mainnet

    Production network

    • Mainnet launch with verifiable inference
    • Validator & compute-node staking goes live
    • Testnet-to-mainnet migration for early builders
    • Ecosystem grant program — first funding round
  6. Q4 2027 — DAO

    Handing over the keys

    • AGIChainAI DAO launch — on-chain governance
    • Treasury control transferred to token holders
    • First community governance proposals & votes
    • 2028+ research agenda published: scaling, privacy-preserving inference, agent-to-agent economies
